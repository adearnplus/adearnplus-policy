Account Deletion Request

Delete Your AdEarn Plus Account

You can delete your AdEarn Plus account directly from inside the app using the delete account option available in the profile or settings section.

If you cannot access the app and still want to delete your account, you can request account deletion manually by sending an email to the address below.

Send your deletion request to: adearnplus@gmail.com

Please include the following in your email request:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your app account details, if available
  • A clear request stating that you want your account deleted

Once your request is verified, your AdEarn Plus account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 days.

Some data may be retained temporarily where required for legal, fraud prevention, security, or compliance purposes.
