1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how AdEarnPlus (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the AdEarnPlus mobile application (the “App”).

2. Information We Collect

  • Account and authentication data: phone number (via OTP), Firebase UID, and basic account metadata.
  • App usage and rewards data: rewarded-ad events, coin balance, cooldown/daily-limit status, and basic fraud-prevention signals.
  • Withdrawal information: withdrawal requests, payout method (UPI or bank), and payout details you provide.
  • Device and diagnostics data: approximate device information (like device model/OS version), crash logs, and performance data (if enabled).
  • Advertising data (AdMob): AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data to serve and measure ads.

3. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account and provide core app features.
  • To credit rewards (coins) after eligible rewarded ad completion.
  • To enforce daily limits, cooldowns, and other rules to prevent abuse and policy violations.
  • To process withdrawal requests and maintain request status (Pending/Approved/Rejected).
  • To monitor, debug, and improve stability and performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

4. Advertising and Rewards

The App includes ads served by Google AdMob. Rewarded ads may grant in-app coins only after the ad is completed and verified by the AdMob SDK.

  • Rewards are subject to verification and may be adjusted or withheld if invalid traffic, abuse, or policy violations are detected.
  • We do not guarantee any fixed earnings or income. Rewards can change without prior notice.
