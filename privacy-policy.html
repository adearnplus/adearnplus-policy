AdEarnPlus is a watch-and-earn mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information (such as email address) when you sign in.
  • Firebase user identifier (UID) used to manage your account.
  • App data such as coin balance, reward history, and usage events.
  • Withdrawal details you provide (such as UPI ID) to request payments.
  • Device and advertising data collected by ad providers (for example, Google AdMob), which may include device identifiers and ad interaction data.

We do not ask for sensitive information such as passwords in plain text, banking PINs, or government ID numbers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide login and account access.
  • To track and update coin rewards after eligible ad views.
  • To process withdrawal requests.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and policy violations.
  • To improve app performance and stability.
  • To show ads and measure ad performance.

3. Third-Party Services

AdEarnPlus uses third-party services that may collect information according to their own policies:

5. Data Security

We store relevant app data using Firebase services and use encrypted connections where applicable. No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect user data.

6. Children’s Privacy

AdEarnPlus is not intended for children under 16. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.
