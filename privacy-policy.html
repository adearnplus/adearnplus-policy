Privacy Policy

App: AdEarn Plus
Effective date: 2026-03-05

This Privacy Policy explains how AdEarn Plus ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Service").

Information we collect

  • Account information: Email address (if you sign up with email), phone number (if you sign in with phone), and user ID (UID).
  • Profile information: Name you set in the app (optional).
  • App usage & rewards data: Coins balance, ads watched count, cooldown timestamps, daily limits, redeem/withdraw request history and status.
  • Device & technical data: Basic device identifiers and diagnostics from Firebase/Google services for security, fraud prevention, and app stability.
  • Advertising data: AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and related signals to serve and measure ads.

How we use information

  • To provide login and account functionality (email, Google sign-in, phone OTP).
  • To track and issue rewards (coins), enforce cooldowns and daily limits, and prevent abuse/fraud.
  • To process redeem/withdraw requests and show their status.
  • To send service notifications (example: redeem updates, system messages, maintenance alerts).
  • To improve the Service, fix bugs, and monitor performance.
  • To comply with legal and policy requirements.

Ads and third-party services

AdEarn Plus uses third-party services that may collect information to provide their features. These include:

  • Google AdMob (ads, measurement, fraud prevention)
  • Firebase Authentication (login)
  • Cloud Firestore (storing user coins, activity, redeem requests)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (push notifications)
  • Firebase Cloud Functions (server-side reward validation)

These services may collect information like device identifiers, IP address, crash logs, and usage events. Their use of information is governed by their own privacy policies.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With service providers (Google/Firebase/AdMob) to operate the Service.
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and prevent fraud.
  • During a business transfer (merger/acquisition), subject to this policy.

Data retention

We retain account and rewards data as long as needed to provide the Service and for legitimate business purposes (fraud prevention, compliance). You can request deletion using the in-app Delete Account option.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards such as Firebase security rules, authentication, and server-side validation (Cloud Functions) to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children's privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 15. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Your choices

  • Access/Update: You can update your name in the app.
  • Delete: You can delete your account from the Profile screen.
  • Notifications: You may control notifications through device settings.
  • Ads: You can reset your advertising ID or limit ad personalization via device settings (where available).

Permissions

The app may request permissions such as INTERNET and POST_NOTIFICATIONS to function. Phone OTP uses Firebase Authentication and does not require reading SMS/Call logs.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will update the effective date above. Continued use of the Service means you accept the updated policy.
